# Product Mission (Lite)

QuarterEndTax Support Utils is an internal web application that helps Tax portfolio support engineers identify and resolve failed quarter-end form and CTR generation jobs by providing a centralised, self-serve operational surface — eliminating the need for direct database or infrastructure access.

It serves Tax portfolio support engineers and platform operations leads who need to act quickly during time-sensitive quarter-end windows. Unlike ad-hoc SQL scripts and undocumented runbooks, QuarterEndTax Support Utils provides a single, environment-aware UI with full audit logging — enabling engineers to find and re-trigger failed CTRs in under 5 minutes, safely toggle Paychex client flags with confirmation dialogs, and maintain a complete immutable record of every support action taken.
